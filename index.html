Welcome to PNC Cognitio School

PNC Educational Trust, founded in 2016 with the aim of establishing a school with global standard and with a mission to allow children to attain very high level of cognitive skills, maintain good physical status and become responsible citizens with mutual respect for fellow beings.

  • PNCCS is currently in the process of obtaining the affiliation to Cambridge International Examinations (CIE).
  • PNC Cognitio School have pledged a strong commitment towards the health of a student.
  • PNCC shares the vision of Partnership for 21st Century Skills in line with Finnish Model.

Leadership

Mr. Ramanjaneya, the CEO and MD of PNC Cognitio is the inspiration behind the institution. Mr. Ramanjaneya is a veteran of the real-estate market in Bangalore. He is also the pioneer behind the Mount Litera school in Whitefield. However, deep down he sought to set up a facility that provides students with educational facilities and global learning methodologies that would be unrivalled anywhere in the country. In his own words his school would be one with “An Indian mission with a Global Vision”. Mr.Ramanjaneya realized that he would need to have the best resources in the education field to achieve this. His pursuit of his dream saw him assemble an array of experienced educationalists who are now the driving force behind his vision to create a school that imparts knowledge in its own distinct way, but also focuses on the students physical well-being, making it the school’s USP.
